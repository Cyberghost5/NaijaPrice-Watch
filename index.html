About NaijaPrice Watch

Empowering Nigerian families with transparent, real-time food market data

🎯

Our Mission

We believe that transparent food price information is a fundamental right. NaijaPrice Watch helps Nigerian families make informed purchasing decisions by providing real-time market data from major food markets across the country.

🌟

Our Vision

To create a Nigeria where food inflation is transparent, budgeting is accessible to all, and every household can afford nutritious meals without financial stress.

💡

Why It Matters

Food prices fluctuate constantly across Nigerian markets. Without reliable data, families overspend and miss opportunities to save. Our platform bridges this gap with accurate, regularly updated information.

⚙️

How It Works

Our team monitors prices at markets in Lagos, Ibadan, and beyond. We collect data daily and feed it into our dashboard. You can then use our smart budget planner to compare prices and plan your shopping wisely.

⭐

Key Features

  • Real-time price tracking
  • Smart budget planning
  • Market-specific data
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • No signup required

Data & Transparency

NaijaPrice Watch is committed to transparency. Our data comes from verified market sources and is updated regularly. Prices may vary by vendor, quantity, and quality. We encourage users to compare prices across vendors and negotiate for better deals. Our goal is to empower you with information, not to replace personal research.
