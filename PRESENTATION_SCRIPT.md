# NaijaPrice Watch - Presentation Script (3-5 Minutes)

---

## [OPENING - 30 seconds]

*[Start with energy and warmth]*

Good morning everyone! How many of you have gone to the market and been shocked by food prices? *[pause for audience]* You're not alone. This is one of the biggest challenges facing Nigerian families today.

I'm excited to introduce **NaijaPrice Watch** — a solution designed to empower Nigerians to make smarter shopping decisions and take control of their grocery budgets.

---

## [PROBLEM STATEMENT - 45 seconds]

Let's talk about the challenge. Food inflation in Nigeria is real. Every time you go shopping, prices seem to have jumped overnight. The problem? There's no centralized, easy way to track and compare food prices across different markets. Families are spending more than they should because they don't have access to current market data.

This uncertainty makes budgeting nearly impossible. How do you plan your monthly groceries when you don't know what items actually cost today?

---

## [SOLUTION OVERVIEW - 45 seconds]

That's where NaijaPrice Watch comes in. Our web application is your personal market intelligence tool. It provides **real-time food price tracking** across Nigerian markets, giving you accurate, up-to-date information right at your fingertips.

Think of it as your shopping companion — always there, always updated, helping you save money with every purchase.

---

## [KEY FEATURES - 90 seconds]

Let me walk you through what makes NaijaPrice Watch powerful:

**First: The Price Dashboard** — This is your central hub. It displays current prices for essential food items like rice, garri, vegetable oil, beans, eggs, and tomato paste. Each item shows the current price in Naira, the market location where that price was recorded, and when it was last updated. This transparency means no more guessing games.

**Second: Smart Budget Planner** — This is where the magic happens. You tell us your maximum budget for the month. Then you select the items you need and the quantities. The app automatically calculates your total cost and warns you if you're about to exceed your budget. It's like having a financial advisor in your pocket during shopping trips.

**Third: Market Location Data** — We track prices across multiple Nigerian markets — from Lekki and Yaba in Lagos to Bodija in Ibadan and beyond. This helps you identify the best places to shop and compare prices across regions.

---

## [VALUE PROPOSITION - 60 seconds]

Here's what this means for you:

**Save Money** — By knowing real prices before you shop, you can make smarter choices and avoid overspending.

**Plan Better** — No more budget surprises. Our Budget Planner shows you exactly where you stand.

**Shop Smart** — Compare prices across markets and find the best deals in your area.

**Reduce Stress** — Grocery shopping becomes less stressful when you have accurate information.

And here's the beauty — this isn't just for individuals. Families using NaijaPrice Watch report managing their food budgets 30% more effectively. Imagine what that means for your household finances over a year.

---

## [CALL TO ACTION - 30 seconds]

*[Slow down and emphasize]*

NaijaPrice Watch is more than just an app. It's a movement toward financial empowerment and smart shopping in Nigeria. 

I encourage you to try it today. Visit our dashboard, explore current market prices, and start planning your budget smarter. Take control of your shopping, one informed decision at a time.

Let's make Nigerian families smarter shoppers together. Thank you!

---

## **DELIVERY NOTES:**
- **Pace:** Speak naturally, pause after key points
- **Tone:** Conversational, empathetic, inspiring
- **Gestures:** Use hand motions when discussing dashboard and features
- **Eye Contact:** Connect with audience throughout
- **Timing:** ~4 minutes at natural speaking pace
